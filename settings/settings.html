Settings Page

This page allows you to configure the button settings on your device. All modifications are saved directly on the device and remain persistent as long as the app is installed. You can reboot the device freely â€” your data will be retained.

  • UDP - Sends the string as is. Feel free to enter any command to be sent.
  • TCP - Sends HEX commands (MDC) to the device, Eg: "AA 13 00 01 00 14" for Mute OFF.
  • Targets - Please add as many targets as desired. separate them all by ','. Eg: 192.168.10.10,192.168.10.11,192.168.10.12

To hide a button from the touchscreen interface, simply leave its Name field empty.

- Please do not add more than one image at a time! This will be corrected shortly!
